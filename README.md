Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy for Assemble: Legendary Companion
Effective date: March 12, 2026

Assemble: Legendary Companion (“the App”) respects your privacy.

Information We Collect
- The App does not require account creation.
- The App does not collect personal information for tracking or advertising.
- Card data you create (such as names, notes, and images) is stored locally on your device.

Camera and Photo Library Access
- If you choose, the App may request access to:
  - Camera: to take photos for card images.
  - Photo Library: to select images for card images.
- These permissions are optional and only used for the features you choose.

Data Sharing
- The App does not sell your data.
- The App does not share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

Data Retention
- Your data remains on your device unless you delete the App or remove data yourself.

Children’s Privacy
- The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
